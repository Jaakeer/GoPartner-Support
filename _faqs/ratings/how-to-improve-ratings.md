title: How can I improve my Partner rating?
category: Ratings
tags: [featured]

You can easily improve the rating given to you by adhering to the following practices
- Make sure the products you deliver has the same quality mentioned in the Purchase order.
- Make sure you ship and deliver the order on the agreed date.
- Make sure the quality of products you deliver is up to the mark, and our clients do not reject the products.
- Do not forget to accept the assigned orders, from the tool in the given period.
- Make sure you create the shipment using the tool, when you are ready to ship the order
